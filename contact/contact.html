Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us

What is the typical response time for inquiries?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support hotline for immediate assistance.

Can I schedule a virtual appointment with a college advisor?

Yes! You can schedule a virtual appointment with one of our college advisors through our online booking system. Simply select "Schedule an Appointment" from the dropdown menu in the form above.

How can I report a technical issue with the website?

To report a technical issue, please select "Technical Support" from the subject dropdown in our contact form. Include details about the problem you're experiencing, such as the device/browser you're using and steps to reproduce the issue.

Are there opportunities to partner with Campus Compass?

We're always open to partnerships that help students achieve their educational goals. Please select "Partnership Opportunities" from the subject dropdown and outline your proposal in the message. Our partnerships team will review and get in touch with you.

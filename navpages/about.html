Our Journey

From a small startup to the premier college guidance platform

2013

The Beginning

Campus Compas was founded by three college graduates who experienced firsthand the challenges of finding the right college match.

2015

First Major Milestone

Reached 100,000 users and partnered with 500 colleges across the country to provide exclusive insights.

2017

AI Integration

Introduced our proprietary AI matching algorithm, improving college recommendations by analyzing thousands of data points.

2019

Global Expansion

Expanded our services to international universities, helping students find opportunities worldwide.

2021

Scholarship Platform

Launched our scholarship matching platform, helping students secure over $30 million in financial aid.

2023

Today

Celebrating 10 years of helping students achieve their educational dreams, with over 2 million success stories.
